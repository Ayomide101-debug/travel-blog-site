Eiffel Tower - Paris

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic structures in the world, located in Paris, France.

Colosseum - Rome

The Colosseum is an ancient amphitheater in the center of Rome, Italy, known for gladiatorial combat.

Statue of Liberty - New York

The Statue of Liberty is a symbol of freedom and democracy, standing on Liberty Island in New York Harbor.

Tokyo Tower - Tokyo

The Tokyo Tower is a famous communications and observation tower in the heart of Tokyo, Japan.

Christ the Redeemer - Rio

Christ the Redeemer is an Art Deco statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.

Pyramids of Giza - Cairo

The Pyramids of Giza are ancient pyramid structures located near Cairo, Egypt, and are the last surviving wonders of the ancient world.
